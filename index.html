Delicious Lo Mein Recipe

Lo Mein is a savory and flavorful Chinese noodle dish that's both comforting and versatile. Made with soft, chewy noodles, fresh vegetables, and a rich, savory sauce, it's a perfect balance of textures and tastes. The combination of tender noodles, crisp vegetables, and aromatic sauces creates a dish that's satisfying and delicious, making it a popular choice for both quick weeknight dinners and special occasions. Whether you prefer it with chicken, beef, shrimp, or simply vegetarian, Lo Mein is a delightful meal that's sure to please any palate.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz. lo mein noodles
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
    •

Sauce:

  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 cup chicken or vegetable broth
    •

Instructions:

  1. Cook the noodles: Boil the lo mein noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, sesame oil, sugar, and broth. Set aside.
  3. Cook the vegetables: Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add mushrooms, bell pepper, carrot, snap peas, and cook for 3-5 minutes until tender.
  4. Add aromatics: Add garlic and ginger to the pan and saut√© for 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Combine noodles and sauce: Add the cooked noodles to the pan and pour the sauce over them. Toss everything together and cook for another 2-3 minutes until heated through.
  6. Serve: Garnish with sliced green onions and serve immediately.
